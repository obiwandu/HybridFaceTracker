Hybridfacetracker

This is a hybrid face tracker consists of a traditional face detector, a kernelized face tracker and missing face re-assignment mechanism.

Effect

This hybrid tracker could track multiple faces and re-assign missing face in real-time.

Tracking

The hybrid face tracker could track multiple faces in a long period of time.

Re-assignment

The hybrid face tracker could re-assign the face when it is missed because of occlusion instead of regarding it as a new face.

Notice the lady's face in the right side, it's labeled as No.4 in a brown bounding box.

Now the lady's face is occluded.

The tracker re-detects this face again and it's still labeled as No.4, not being regarded as a new face.
